Fuzzy control for start-up of an isothermal CSTR (2004)

(1) The author pointed out that there are many difficulties for controlling nonlinear process:

  1. dealing with disturbance
  2. changing setpoint
  3. Only a few efforts reported dealing with automatic start-up. Controllers found inadequate without specific strategies

(2) Fuzzy Logic Control (unnecessary)

  1. Require human knowledge or human experiences (what should operator adjust when facing unacceptable situations)
  2. Work without a mathematical model
  3. Requirement of many parameters when tuning

Fuzzy set…… Defuzzied……

(3) Start-up operation

In this case, the reaction is A+B→C+D, product is C. In other word, feed A and B and discharge A, B, C, D.

Here are the stages in start-up operation:

  1. Filling up (in this stage fill up the tank while heating up at a certain rate. so the residence time changes)
  2. Approaching steady state
  3. Steady state

(4) The product concentration decreases with increasing start-up flow rate

Thoughts derived from this paper:

  1. Bring controlled variables at desired value during stage 1
  2. Control feed are proportional at a determined ratio

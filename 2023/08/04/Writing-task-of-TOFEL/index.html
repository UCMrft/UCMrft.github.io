Writing task of TOFEL

Posted on 2023-08-04 In Language Learning

Independent Writing

A. Composed of 4 or 5 paragraphs: introduction,2-3 body paragraphs and a conclusion.

B. In introduction part, it is necessary to straightly point out general statement and thesis of the topic. General statement better not include personal opinion. Thesis, which contains personal prospective, is the main feature of introduction.

C. Each major piece of supports is a topic sentence for one of the body paragraphs. Each topic sentence is followed by explanations that show why or how the information in your topic supports your thesis.

D. Conclusion restate main ideas and explain how the connect to the thesis. Try not to repeat words or sentences showed in paragraphs above.

Actually there are 3 types: agree or disagree / comparisons / personal opinions to social issues

In beginning, present information broadly. Thesis is the answer but do not include minor details.

…….

Example 1

Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? People in the past ate healthier food than people today do. Use specific reasons and examples to support your responses.

Response

There is a debate that whether people eat healthier today or in the past. Someone support the idea that people in the past ate healthier food than people today do. From my perspective , I am not in favor of this idea when considering food safety, science of diet and, most significantly, accessibility and diversity of food.

Food safety is easy to understand. People in the past didn’t have technologies to get rid of insects or bacteria exist in food, which may cause severe diseases and even death. It is obviously convert to the definition of health because people in the past are exposed in food risk without the protection of technologies.

Reasonable diet is becoming an important role in daily lives. People can acquire better mental state and body condition relying on proper diet arrangement. People in the past may not have this advanced concept. They probably just fetch anything accessible and eat to kill hunger regardless of what these can bring to their body. People in high class could chase for extreme lavish menu to express their premier status. A healthy diet can only be a fairy tale for them.

Diversity and accessibility of food also important. In the past, people can only ate food harvested in contemporary area or season. For example, someone can not taste the sweetness of apple in summer, or imperial concubine could not suddenly across entire China to eat lichee.

To sum up, a lot of technologies and concepts had been developed and proposed which are beneficial to the health of human beings…….
